coll_tokenize¶

vsm.corpus.util.corpusbuilders.coll_tokenize(books, book_names)¶

coll_tokenize is a helper function for coll_corpus().

Takes a list of books and book_names, and returns words and corpus data.

Parameters:
  • books (list) – List of books.
  • book_names (list) – List of book names.
Returns:

words : List of words. words in the books tokenized by word_tokenize(). corpus_data : Dictionary with context type as keys and corresponding tokenizations as values. The tokenizations are np.arrays.
