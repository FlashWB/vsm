Here are the viewer classes that correspond to each model. These viewer objects include model-specific functions to visualize semantic distance between words, documents, and topics (for LDA).

Beagle viewer - used with any variations of the Beagle model.

LDA Gibbs viewer - used with models LDA Gibbs or LDA CGS Multi.

LSA viewer - used with LSA model model.

Tf viewer - used with models Tf Seq or LDA CGS Multi.

TfIdf viewer - used with the Tf-Idf model.

Now, Take your vsm.corpus.Corpus and vsm.model to explore your corpus using the viewer classes.
